Welcome to SVERI College of Engineering

SVERI's College of Engineering (COE), Pandharpur is a premier institute established in 1998 under the aegis of Shri Vithal Education and Research Institute (SVERI), Pandharpur. The institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere.
